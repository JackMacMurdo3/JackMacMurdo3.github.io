About

Michael Jordan is a 6-6 shooting guard. He is right handed and is 198 lbs. He was born on February 17, 1963. Jordan is now 59 years old.

Achievements

Michael Jordan is the greatest basketball player of all time. Jordan has won 5 MVPs (1988, 1991, 1992, 1996, 1998), 6 NBA Championships (1991, 1992, 1993, 1996, 1997, 1998), 6 Finals MVPs (1991, 1992, 1993, 1996, 1997, 1998),NBA Rookie of the Year (1984-1985), 14 Time NBA All Star, 11 All-NBA Selections. Jordan has a 100% win percentage in the NBA Finals. He was inducted into the Hall of Fame in 2009.

Teams

Jordan has played for 2 teams. The Chicago Bulls and the Washington Wizards. In college, he played for the university of North Carolina.

Stats

Jordan averaged 30.1 points, 6.2 rebounds, and 5.3 assists per game. During his career he had a 49.7 field goal percentage, 32.7 from 3, and 83.5 from the free throw line.
