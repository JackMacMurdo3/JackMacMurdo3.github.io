About

Lionel Messi is a 5-7 forward. He is left-footed and 148 lbs. Messi has a size 10 shoe and was born on June 24, 1987 (35 years old).

Achievements

2022 World Cup Champion. Messi has recieved 7 Ballon d'Ors (2009, 2010, 2011, 2012, 2015, 2019, 2021), 4 Champions League Titles (2006, 2009, 2011, 2015), 10 LaLiga Titles (2005, 2006, 2009, 2010, 2011, 2013, 2015, 2016, 2018, 2019), 9 LaLiga MVP (2009, 2010, 2011, 2012, 2013, 2017, 2018, 2019), FIFA World Player (2009), 6 Champions Top Scorer (2009, 2010, 2011, 2012, 2015, 2019).

Teams

Messi has played for 3 different teams in his professional career. 2003-2021 he played for FC Barcelona. In 2021-present for PSG. From 2005-present he has played for Argentina (his home country).

Stats

Messi has scored a total of 789 goals, 348 assists, in 1000 appearances. 104 minutes per goal and 72 minutes per goal contribution. 129 Champions League goals and 40 assists. 7 goals and 5 assists in 20 World Cup appearances.
