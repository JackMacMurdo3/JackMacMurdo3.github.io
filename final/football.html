About

Tom Brady is a 6-4 quarterback. He throws with his right hand and is 225 lbs. He went to Michigan for College and was born on August 3, 1977 (45 years old).

Achievements

7 time Super Bowl Champion (2002, 2004, 2005, 2015, 2017, 2019, 2021), 3 time All-Pro(2007, 2010, 2017), 15 time Pro Bowl most in NFL history, and best Male Athlete ESPY Award 2021.

Teams

Tom Brady has played football for two professional teams. The New England Patriots and the Tampa Bay Buccaneers. During college he played for Michigan.

Stats

Tom Brady has an overall record of 249-79, 64.3 pass completion percentage, 87852 yards, 640 touchdowns, 206 interceptions.
